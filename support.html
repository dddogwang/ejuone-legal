FAQ

Q: How do I restore my EJUOne Pro purchase on a new device?

A: Go to Settings > EJUOne Pro > Restore Purchases. Make sure you are signed in with the same Apple ID used for the original purchase.

Q: How do I sync my study data across devices?

A: EJUOne Pro users can sign in with the same account on multiple devices. Your study data will automatically sync via the cloud.

Q: How do I delete my account?

A: Go to Settings > Account > Delete Account. Please note that this action is irreversible and all your cloud data will be permanently deleted.

Q: I found an error in a question. How can I report it?
