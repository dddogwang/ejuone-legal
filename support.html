Frequently Asked Questions

How do I restore my EJUOne Pro purchase on a new device?
Go to Settings → EJUOne Pro → Restore Purchases. Make sure you are signed in with the same Apple ID used for the original purchase.
How do I sync my study data across devices?
EJUOne Pro users can sign in with the same account on multiple devices. Your study data will automatically sync via the cloud.
How do I delete my account?
Go to Settings → Account → Delete Account. Please note that this action is irreversible and all your cloud data will be permanently deleted.
I found an error in a question. How can I report it?
Please send an email to dddogwang@gmail.com with the subject, year, and question number. We appreciate your help in improving the App.
Is EJUOne Pro a subscription?
No. EJUOne Pro is a one-time purchase that grants permanent access — no recurring charges.
